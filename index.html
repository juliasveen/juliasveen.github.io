Attendance Case Study - Dodger Stadium Analysis

Language(s): R

I conducted a data analysis project on Dodgers Stadium attendance patterns from 2014 to 2022 for my Applied Data Analytics in Sports course. Using R, I managed the entire process—including cleaning and merging datasets—to build regression models that identified how team performance, timing, and specific opponents impact stadium capacity. This project provided real-world insights to help the Dodgers organization optimize their marketing and ticket strategies.

Amazon Redesign Prototype

Canva & Powerpoint

For this UX Design project, I developed a mock high-fidelity prototype for an Amazon redesign focused on streamlining the navigation and personalizing the user journey. By restructuring the information architecture, I introduced a more intuitive "Shop by Department" drop-down menu and integrated automatic AI suggestions based on real-time browsing history to enhance product discoverability. This project allowed me to tackle the complexities of a large-scale e-commerce platform, refining it into a cleaner, more user-centric prototype that prioritizes ease of use and engagement.

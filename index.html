Attendance Case Study - Dodger Stadium Analysis

As someone passionate about baseball, I conducted a data analysis project on attendance patterns at Dodgers Stadium from 2014 to 2022 for my Applied Data Analytics in Sports course. My goal was to determine how factors like team performance, specific opponents, and seasonal trends impacted the percentage of stadium capacity filled. I was responsible for the entire analysis process, including data cleaning, merging datasets, and building statistical models in R. I developed multiple regression models to predict attendance trends and evaluated their accuracy using R-squared values, MAE, and RMSE. This project allowed me to apply my data analysis skills to a real-world scenario and provide insights that could help optimize attendance strategies.
